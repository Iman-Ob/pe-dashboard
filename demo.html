PROMPT DASHBOARD

9 templates. Fill → Preview → Export.

Structured prompt engineering using the most effective frameworks for AI training.



HOW TO USE
01
Choose a Template
From the home screen, select one of the 9 structured templates — RTF, TAG, BAB, CARE, RISE, TREF, GRADE, MAX, or META. Each one is designed for a specific prompting goal.
02
Fill the Fields
Type your content into each field. The assembled prompt updates live on the right panel as you type — no button needed.
[ROLE]
You are a senior instructional designer...

[TASK]
Create a 3-hour AI literacy workshop agenda...

[FORMAT]
Time-blocked table with objectives per session.
03
Copy or Export
Click Copy to paste the prompt directly into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI tool. Or click Export to download all 9 templates as a formatted Excel file for offline handoff.
04
Sync with Google Sheets
Click ⇄ Sync on any template to push your data to Google Sheets — or pull updates from co-trainers. All data persists locally between sessions automatically.
// Settings → Paste your Apps Script URL once
SCRIPT_URL = "https://script.google.com/macros/s/.../exec"

// Then: Push ↑ or Pull ↓ any template
PROMPT LIBRARY

Your team's prompt knowledge base.

Add, organize, rate and version every prompt your team creates. Live sync keeps everyone updated.

⌕
Search & Filter
Search by title, text, tags, or use case. Filter by category, template type, or minimum star rating. Results update instantly.
★
Star Ratings
Rate every prompt 1–5 stars. Filter by minimum rating to surface only your best-performing prompts during sessions.
⑂
Version History
Every edit saves as a new version. View the full history, click any version to preview it, and track what changed between iterations.
⇄
Live Team Sync
Push your prompts to Google Sheets so co-trainers can pull the latest. Auto-sync every 1, 5, or 10 minutes keeps everyone aligned.
ع
Bilingual EN / AR
Switch the entire interface between English (LTR) and Arabic (RTL) with one click. Dubai font renders both scripts perfectly.
📲
Works as App
Installable as a PWA on Android and desktop. Runs offline after first load — no internet required during training sessions.

01
Add a Prompt
Click "+ Add Prompt". Fill the title, prompt text, category, template type, tags, use case, author, and star rating. Click Save — it pushes to Google Sheets automatically.
02
Find What You Need
Use the search bar or sidebar filters. Click any tag on a card to filter by that tag instantly. Sort by newest, highest rated, or most used.
03
Use It
Open a prompt card to see the full text, metadata, and version history. Click Copy to grab the text instantly. Each open auto-increments the usage counter.
